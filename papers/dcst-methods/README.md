## dcst-methods

Proposed home for a cross-application dCST methods manuscript that combines:

- gut external-transfer and portability results from the current gut-dCST paper
- selected CT outcome-association results from the CT clearance manuscript
- a minimal methods framing derived from the original Linf theory paper

Suggested purpose:

- make the case that dCSTs are a deterministic, interpretable, sample-level representation
- show that the framework is useful in more than one microbiome niche and more than one analysis mode
- keep the paper methods-forward without turning it into a full mathematical theory manuscript

Recommended top-level structure:

- `manuscript/` for the new manuscript source
- `notes/` for outlines, decisions, and migration plans
- `assets/` for manuscript-facing figures and tables
- `scripts/` for figure/table builders specific to this manuscript
- `build/` for local compiled outputs
- `archive/` for superseded drafts or imported legacy fragments
